# Whole Foods Market × Amazon
## Worldwide Grocery Strategy & Business Performance Analytics

Strategic analytics dashboard analyzing 6 years of Whole Foods 
Market business performance post-Amazon acquisition (2019–2024).

## Overview
Built using real data from Amazon Annual Reports and SEC filings 
to uncover insights across 8 analytical views.

## Key Findings
- Revenue grew from $17.2B to $21.2B (2019–2024)
- Prime membership drives 60% of all WFM transactions
- Online orders exploded from 8.2M to 32.4M
- 530 stores across US, Canada, and UK
- Gross margin of 34.5% outperforms conventional grocery average by 12.5pp

## Data Sources
- Amazon Annual Reports (2019–2024)
- SEC Form 8-K Filings
- Industry Benchmark Reports
- Progressive Grocer

## Tech Stack
- Tableau (dashboard & visualization)
- SQL (data preparation & pipeline)
- Python (pandas, data cleaning)
- Excel (financial modeling)
